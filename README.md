# Data Visualization and Storytelling

## Tool Used
Power BI Desktop

## Dataset
Superstore Sales Dataset (9,994 orders, 21 columns)

## Objective
Create visualizations that convey a compelling business story
using sales data from a retail superstore.

## Charts Created
1. **Bar Chart** - Sales by Customer Segment
   - Consumer segment leads with 50.56% of total sales

2. **Line Chart** - Monthly Sales Trend
   - Sales peak in August and February every year

3. **Scatter Chart** - Impact of Discount on Profit
   - High discounts (>50%) consistently result in losses

4. **Line Chart** - Yearly Sales Growth
   - 52% sales growth from 2014 to 2017

5. **Pie Chart** - Sales Distribution by Category
   - Technology leads at 36.4% of total revenue

## Key Business Insights
- Consumer customers drive the most revenue (50.56%)
- Heavy discounting hurts profitability
- Strong seasonal pattern — peak sales in August & February
- Consistent year-over-year growth across all categories
